ABOUT US

Power Spot is building the infrastructure for on-the-go charging across East Africa. Starting in Mogadishu, we're deploying smart charging stations at the locations you already visit — malls, cafés, hotels, airports, and universities.

Our power banks come with built-in cables for every device. Just scan the QR code, take a power bank, and return it to any station when you're done. No app needed. No hassle.
